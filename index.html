    Strategies

    Crypto Algo

    Advanced momentum recognition takes advantage of wide movements in the largest, most accessible crypto names. Multiple momentum based indicators have been fully optimized and rigorously tested to produce impressive results regardless of whether it goes up or down.

    Recent Notable Crypto Algo Decisions:

    • Shorting ETH in December of 2021 due to momentum shift to downside
    • Buying ETH in October of 2020 prior to run from $380 to $4,000+

    Stock Algo

    Deep analysis of various macro assets and their historic correlation levels provides tremendous insight into the anticipated strength of the stock market. Based on this automated analysis, the Stock Algo will determine whether it should hold risky ETF’s that benefit from market strength or hold ETF’s that benefit from market weakness.

    Recent Notable Stock Algo Decisions:

    • Selling stocks prior to the 2020 covid crash due to unusual dip in industrial metals
    • Aggressively holding bullish leveraged index ETF’s during the 2019 bull run due to recognized market strength
